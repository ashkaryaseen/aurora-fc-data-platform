# Business Process – Training Day

## Scenario

Aurora FC completes its daily training session.

### Step 1 – Training Session

Players complete the scheduled training session.

**Data Generated**

- Attendance
- Training drills
- Session duration
- Coach observations

---

### Step 2 – Performance Tracking

GPS trackers and wearable devices automatically upload performance data.

**Data Generated**

- Distance covered
- Sprint count
- Top speed
- High-intensity runs
- Acceleration and deceleration

---

### Step 3 – Medical Assessment

Medical staff evaluate players after training.

**Data Generated**

- Heart rate
- Recovery score
- Injury status
- Muscle soreness
- Fitness assessment

---

### Step 4 – Data Ingestion

The Aurora Data Platform automatically ingests all training-related data into the raw data layer.

---

### Step 5 – Data Validation

The platform validates incoming data.

Examples:

- Missing player IDs
- Duplicate records
- Invalid timestamps
- Missing GPS values

---

### Step 6 – Data Transformation

The validated data is transformed into business-ready datasets.

Examples:

- Player workload
- Weekly training load
- Fitness trends
- Recovery metrics

---

### Step 7 – Analytics

The platform generates dashboards for different departments.

Head Coach

- Training intensity
- Player workload

Medical Team

- Injury risk
- Recovery status

Performance Analysts

- Sprint analysis
- Physical performance

---

### Step 8 – Executive Reporting

Weekly reports are automatically generated for the Sporting Director and CEO.

These reports summarize:

- Squad fitness
- Injury trends
- Training intensity
- Overall performance indicators
