# Business Requirements

## Problem Statement

Aurora FC currently operates with data spread across multiple departments and disconnected systems. This makes it difficult to obtain reliable insights and slows decision-making.

## Objective

Build a centralized Data Platform that enables every department within Aurora FC to access trusted data and make better decisions.

## Vision

At Aurora FC, every decision should be backed by trusted data.

## Business Goals

- Improve player performance through data-driven insights.
- Reduce injuries by monitoring player workload.
- Support scouting with performance analytics.
- Improve tactical analysis for coaches.
- Increase commercial revenue through business analytics.
- Build a scalable platform capable of supporting AI-powered decision making.
