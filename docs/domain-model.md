# Aurora FC Domain Model

## Football Operations

### Player

Represents a registered football player contracted by Aurora FC.

### Team

Represents Aurora FC squads (First Team, U21, Academy).

### Match

Represents an official football match.

### Training Session

Represents a scheduled training activity.

### GPS Session

Stores player tracking data captured during training or matches.

### Medical Record

Stores player medical assessments and injury history.

### Scouting Report

Stores scouting observations and player evaluations.

### Competition

Represents leagues, cups, and tournaments.

---

## Business Operations

### Fan

Represents a registered supporter.

### Ticket

Represents a purchased match ticket.

### Merchandise Order

Represents purchases from the club store.

### Sponsor

Represents commercial partnerships.
